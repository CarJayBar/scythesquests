Quest Rules:

The player has one day to complete all of the quests.
He may use one of the following life lines:

  • Phone a friend
  • Google research
  • Ask loving girlfriend to assist

Upon completion of each quest, the player will be rewarded with adoration, and various displays of affection from loving girlfriend.

Once all the quests have completed, the player will be rewarded with a meal, the oppourtunity to put his boerewors into a bun (which has been cut through the top), followed by some well-earned gaming time.

The player has the choice between a co-op, split screen game involving two animated voodoo dolls made by a child, a souls game, a racing game, or an RPG game of his choosing.

The player may use the gaming console of his choosing.

The option of pipe smoking is allowed, if the player so chooses.

The player is encouraged to drive like a normal person between each quest, as to not fling loving girlfriend around the inside of the white chariot.
